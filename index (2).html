Our Story

25 Years of
Redefining Possible.

From a single campus event to Asia's largest technology festival — Techfest's journey mirrors humanity's relentless march toward the impossible.

1998 — The Beginning
Born at IIT Bombay
A small group of engineers dreamed of a space where science and creativity could collide. Techfest launched with 12 events and a handful of participants — all from India. The spark was lit.
2005 — Going Global
International Footprint
Techfest crossed borders for the first time, welcoming teams from 12 countries and forging partnerships with MIT, Stanford, and ETH Zurich. The world started paying attention.
2012 — Record Breaking
Asia's Largest Title
75,000+ participants. 120+ events. 38 countries. Techfest earned its recognition as Asia's largest science and technology festival — a title it has defended every year since.
2020 — Adapting to Change
Techfest Goes Digital
A global pandemic could not stop Techfest. We pioneered Asia's first large-scale virtual tech festival — reaching 1.2M+ viewers across 72 countries without missing a single edition.
2025 — The Future
The Next Chapter
Techfest 2025 arrives with its most ambitious program ever — 300+ events, 56 countries, an AI-powered experience platform, and a new generation of builders, dreamers, and doers.
